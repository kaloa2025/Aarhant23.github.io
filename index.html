AARHANT'23

Guardians of the virtual realm

The Department of Cyber Security, RCOEM is organizing Aarhant, a dynamic cybersecurity awareness event designed to equip young college students with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the digital landscape securely Through engaging workshops, seminars, and fun games.

.

" Guardians of the virtual realm "

Our primary goal is to simplify and clarify the often intimidating world of cybersecurity, making it accessible and relevant to students from diverse technical backgrounds. By attending Aarhant, participants can expect to embark on a transformative journey towards a holistic understanding of cybersecurity. Our carefully crafted programs and workshops cater to beginners and tech-savvy individuals alike, ensuring that everyone leaves with a comprehensive grasp of the subject.
Beyond just enhancing personal online safety, this knowledge equips students with valuable skills that are increasingly essential in today's digital age.

ABOUT

Head of Department

Dr. Rashmi Welekar

Dr. Rashmi Welekar, holds the esteemed position of Head of the Department at Cybersecurity, Ramdeobaba College of Engineering and Management. In AARHANT'23, her substantial expertise plays a pivotal role in ensuring that cybersecurity becomes accessible and enjoyable to all the participants of AARHANT'23.

Our Mission

AARHANT'23 is dedicated to the elevation of cybersecurity awareness. The event's core mission revolves around equipping students with crucial knowledge, skills, and comprehensive insights into the advantages and drawbacks of cybersecurity.

Past Events

Previously, under the banner of AARHANT, notable events including cybersecurity workshops and AARHANT'22 have been orchestrated. These gatherings have effectively served as invaluable platforms for both learning and networking.

Note

AARHANT'23 remains unwavering in its commitment to fostering awareness of cybersecurity in a concise yet highly impactful manner. Your participation in this essential journey is encouraged and welcomed!

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

How can one register for the cybersecurity event?

To register for the event, simply visit our registration page and follow the easy steps to secure your spot. You can also find our registration desk at IT Square during college time.

What is the dress code for the event?

There is a formal attire requirement only for the MUN event, otherwise we encourage you to dress comfortably.

Is this event open to all skill levels?

Yes, our cybersecurity event welcomes participent of all skill levels, from beginners to experts. We offer a range of activities and workshops suitable for everyone.

What is the event schedule?

You can find the detailed event schedule on the "Agenda" page of our website. It includes information about keynote speakers, breakout session, and networking opportunities.

Is there a fee to attend the cybersecurity event?

Our event offers both free and paid registration options. Please review the registration page for pricing details and what each option includes.
